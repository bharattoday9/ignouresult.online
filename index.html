Some Important Questions asked by Students

  1. What is the difference between the study material and the assignment?
    • The study material is a book that contains the course content. The assignment is a question paper that contains questions based on the course content.
  2. What is the difference between the previous year papers and the study material?
    • The previous year papers are the question papers that were asked in the previous years. The study material is a book that contains the course content.
  3. What is the difference between the previous year papers and the assignment?
    • The previous year papers are the question papers that were asked in the previous years. The assignment is a question paper that contains questions based on the course content.
  4. Are the answers given accurate?
    • Yes, the answers are accurate. We have double-checked the answers before publishing them.
  5. Who writes the answers?
    • The answers are written by the students who have already completed the course. They have written the answers based on their experience.

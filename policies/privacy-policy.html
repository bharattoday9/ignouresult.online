Privacy Policy

12 Dec, 2022 | Written by Naman Kumar

Here at ignouresult.online we understand that your privacy is important to you. We want you to feel comfortable and confident when using our services, so we've put together this Privacy Policy to explain our practices regarding the collection, use, disclosure, and protection of your information.

1. Collection of Information

We may collect information from you in a variety of ways, including when you visit our website, submit information to us, or interact with our services. This information may include, but is not limited to, your name, email address, phone number, and payment information. We may also collect non-personally identifiable information, such as your IP address, browser type, operating system, and other usage data.

2. Use of Information

We may use the information we collect from you in a variety of ways, including to provide our services, improve our services, respond to inquiries, personalize your experience, and communicate with you. We understand the importance of protecting your personal data, which belongs to you, not us. The following will provide you with information about what data we collect and the reasons for it, when we access your information, and what rights you have.

3. Disclosure of Information

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personally identifiable information to outside parties. We may disclose non-personally identifiable information to outside parties for research and analysis purposes.

4. Protection of Information

We employ reasonable measures to protect the security of your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

5. Advertising and Analytics Services

We may advertise our content on platforms like Facebook, Google, etc to reach more users. In such cases, it is important for us to measure the user activity and hence we may collect additional data as per the necessity. We may also use third-party analytics services to measure the user activity on our website. These services may collect additional data as per the necessity.

6. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time, and we will notify you of any material changes.

7. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@ignouresult.online.

8. A Funny Note

We take your privacy seriously, but we don't take ourselves too seriously. That's why we include this funny note to let you know that we appreciate a good laugh now and then! So if you have a funny joke, a hilarious meme, or a funny story, please share it with us! We'd love to hear it.

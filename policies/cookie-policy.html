Cookie Policy

This website uses cookie(s) to improve your user experience. By using this website, you agree to the use of cookies. You can find more information about cookies in our Privacy Policy.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work, or work more efficiently, as well as to provide information to the owners of the site.

What cookies do we use?

We use cookies to make our website work and to collect information about how you use our website. We use this information to improve our website and services. We also use cookies to provide you with a better experience when you use our website. Some of the cookies we use are essential for the site to work.

For an instance, we use cookies to remember your preferences, such as your preferred language, and to remember your cookie preferences. We also use cookies to help us understand how people use our website, such as which pages are the most popular, and if we get any error messages from our users. We use this information to improve our website and services.

We may also display ads (our main source of income) on our website. These ads may use cookies to collect information about your visit to our website. This information may be used to provide you with more relevant ads. We do not control these cookies, and they are not subject to this Cookie Policy.

We use Analytics cookies to collect information about how you use our website. We use this information to improve our website and services. We also use cookies to provide you with a better experience when you use our website. Some of the cookies we use are essential for the site to work.

How to control cookies

You can control and/or delete cookies as you wish – for details, see aboutcookies.org. You can delete all cookies that are already on your computer and you can set most browsers to prevent them from being placed. If you do this, however, you may have to manually adjust some preferences every time you visit a site and some services and functionalities may not work.

Third-party cookies

In some special cases we also use cookies provided by trusted third parties. The following section details which third party cookies you might encounter through this site.

This site uses Google Analytics, which is one of the most widespread and trusted analytics solutions on the web for helping us to understand how you use the site and ways that we can improve your experience. These cookies may track things such as how long you spend on the site and the pages that you visit so we can continue to produce engaging content. For more information on Google Analytics cookies, see the official Google Analytics page.

We use advertising cookies to collect information about how you use our website. We use this information to improve our website and services. We also use cookies to provide you with a better experience when you use our website. Some of the cookies we use are essential for the site to work.

SUMMARY

At IGNOUResult.Online, we use two types of cookies:

  1. Functional Cookies: These are necessary for the proper functioning of our website. We use these cookies to remember your preferences while you’re using our website, so you don’t have to re-enter them each time you visit.
  2. Advertising Cookies: These are used to show you relevant ads based on the pages you’ve visited. We also use these cookies for analytics purposes, so we can track how many people view our ads.

How Long Do Our Cookies Last?

Our cookies last for up to one year, depending on the type of cookie. Once the cookie expires, it will be deleted from your computer.

How Do I Opt Out of Our Cookies?

If you don’t want to accept our cookies, you can opt out at any time by changing the settings on your browser. Each browser has different steps for doing this, so please consult your browser’s “Help” section for more information. Thanks for taking the time to read our Cookies Policy.

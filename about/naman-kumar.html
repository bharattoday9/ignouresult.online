About Naman Kumar

12 Dec, 2022 | Written by Naman Kumar

Naman Kumar is the founder and Manager of ignouresult.online website.

Hi there friends. I am Naman Kumar, the founder and Manager of ignouresult.online.

I am a student of IGNOU and I am pursuing my BA History Hons. degree from this university. I am a passionate blogger and I love to write about technology, blogging, and SEO. I am also a web developer and I love to develop websites and web applications.

I am also a part-time freelancer and I love to work on Fiverr. I have been working on Fiverr for the last 2 years and I have completed more than 1000 orders on Fiverr. I am also a part-time freelancer on Upwork and I have completed more than 100 orders on Upwork.

Purpose of this website

While I am studying at IGNOU (in my 3rd Year; to be completed in July 2023), I have been facing a lot of problems regarding the answers to academic questions (like assignments, term-end exams, etc.). I have been searching for the answers to these questions on the internet, but I was not able to find the answers to my questions. The ones that are available are not upto the mark!!

So I decided to create a website that provides answers to all the questions asked in the IGNOU books, exams and assignments. I have been working on this website for the last 2 years and I have completed the website in 2021. I have also added a lot of features to this website, which makes it a great resource for IGNOU students.

I have asked my seniors (alumni of IGNOU) to help me in this project. They have helped me a lot in this project and I am very thankful to them. I have also asked my friends to help me in this project. They have also helped me a lot in this project and I am very thankful to them. Besides, two of my cousins are Civil Servants and they have also helped me a lot in this project. I am very thankful to them.

Thank you

I am extremely thankful to you for visiting this website. I hope that you will find this website useful and helpful. If you have any queries or suggestions, please feel free to contact me. I will be happy to help you.
